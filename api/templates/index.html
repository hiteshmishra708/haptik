    About Us

    Haptik was born out of a personal need when we realized we were using text communication more than any other application on our phones, but still had to use voice for brands and services we use. We also realized that it’s generally a pain to find any information related to such companies, and would like to be more in the loop on their products. We asked a few friends and advisers, and they agreed. We asked some more, and they agreed too. #hellohaptik






    Aakrit Vaish
    Co-Founder & CEO

    Aakrit is responsible for Haptik’s day-to-day operations, based in the Mumbai office. Before he dreamt up this crazy idea, he was one of the early guys at Flurry where he wore a bunch of hats and was instrumental in growing the company from a startup to a multi-million dollar business. Aakrit also invests in technology startups in India and sits on the boards of Flat.to and Winmark Enterprises. He got a BS in Industrial Engineering from the University of Illinois, Urbana-Champaign. Outside of work, Aakrit is an avid sports follower and always up for talking about Manchester United, the Indian cricket team, the Illini, 49ers, the Bulls & Roger Federer.

    Harish Anand Thilakan
    Design & Marketing

    Harish looks after overall design and marketing at Haptik, in a consulting capacity. Based out of Chennai, he founded and continues to be Managing Partner at Influx Interactive, one of India's leading digital marketing agencies. Since 2005, Influx has provided creative design and web marketing services to some of India's largest brands including Reliance Communications, Big FM, Zapak, Kingfisher Airlines, Royal Enfield, PVR Cinemas, INOX Leisure and Apollo Hospitals. Harish also consults with one of South India's leading saree brands in their pursuit of creating India's first international saree brand - Palam Silks. Along the way, Harish has picked up a keen interest in single malts, cars, watches and fashion brands.

    Miten Sampat
    Adviser

    Miten is a friend and adviser to Haptik, helping them think through strategy and operations. He is currently GM & Business Head at TimesCity, India's leading destination for consumers to find restaurants, nightlife, movies, and events in their cities. Prior to TimesCity, Miten was based in San Francisco, where he was VP of Product Strategy at Neustar, enabling some of the largest web companies understand “where” their users come from. Before that, Miten was Chief Architect & CTO at Feeva where he developed privacy friendly online advertising technology for Internet operators. Miten has a BS & MS in Computer Science from Virginia Tech.

    Swapan Rajdev

    Swapan is responsible for all the technology magic that happens at Haptik, based in San Francisco.
